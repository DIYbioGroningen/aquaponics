#Prototype
This prototype will be a prove of concept for the mechanism, it will not have all the functions.

**What it will have:**
* Water + nutrient distribution
* Controlled grow lights
* Soil moisture sensor
* Temperature sensor
* Real Time Clock
* Feedback via LEDs
